* Adding the ability to configure vertical margins (top and bottom) with optional fading
* Allowing the changing of text and background color of the prompter
* Adding darkmode and allowing a user to chose the primary color of the app (because that's cool)
* Sail the seas with TiefPrompt! English (pirate) is now supported! Hoist yer anchor ta set to sea!
