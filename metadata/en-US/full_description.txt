TiefPrompt is an extraordinarily simple teleprompter app for creators, presenters, and professionals. 

Load your script from a file or paste it directly, then control playback with play, pause, speed adjustment, and orientation flip options.

TiefPrompt is open-source and available across platforms. Perfect for video recording, live presentations, and speech practice.

Fully featured with adjustable fonts, custom colors, and keyboard shortcuts to all your likings. Take control of your teleprompter with the no-distraction interface.
