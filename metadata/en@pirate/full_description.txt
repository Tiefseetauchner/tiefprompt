Tiefprompt be crafted t’ give ye a simple ‘n’ customizable scrollin’ text voyage fer yer presentin’, recordin’ o’ moving pictures, and shoutin’ grand speeches. It comes with th’ basics ye need t’ steer yer readin’ and command th’ scroll with ease.

What be aboard, but not limited to:
* Keyboard Helm Control: Steer and command yer scrollin’ with the tappin’ of keys, like a true navigator.
* Readin’ Beacons: Glimmerin’ signs t’ help yer eyes stay the course o’ the text.
* Custom Fonts Ahoy: Tweak th’ look and hue o’ yer letters t’ fit yer own pirate taste.
* Script Stowage: Save yer scrolls, toss ‘em overboard when they’re no longer needed.

The app sails light, swift, and free o’ clutterin’ barnacles.
