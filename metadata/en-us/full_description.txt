Tiefprompt is designed to provide a simple and customizable scrolling text experience for presentations, video recording, and speeches. It includes essential features for adjusting the reading experience and controlling playback in a convenient way.

Features include but are not limited to:
* Keyboard Control: Navigate and control playback using a keyboard.
* Reading Indicators: Visual indicators to assist with tracking text while reading.
* Customizable Fonts: Change the font style to suit personal preferences.
* Save and Manage Scripts: Store custom scripts and delete them when no longer needed.

The app aims to be lightweight, efficient, and free of unnecessary distractions.
